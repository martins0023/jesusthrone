RCCG VISION/MISSION STATEMENT

The Mission Statements of The Redeemed Christian Church of God.

1. To make Heaven.
2. To take as many people as possible.
3. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
4. To achieve No.1, Holiness must be our lifestyle.
5. To accomplish No.2 & 3, we will plant churches within 5 minutes walking distance in every city and town in developing nations and 5 minutes driving distance in developed countries.
6. To pursue these objectives until every nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.
